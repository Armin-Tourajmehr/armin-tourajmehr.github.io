About me

Welcome!
I’m a Master’s graduate in Computer Science (Data Science) from the Tehran Institute for Advanced Studies (Teias). I was fortunate to complete my master's thesis under the supervision of Dr. Yadollah Yaghoobzadeh, where my research focused on evaluating the creativity of LLMs in Persian literary text generation. Throughout my graduate studies, I worked on various machine learning and natural language processing projects and gained valuable hands-on experience. Currently, I serve as a Research Assistant at the Teias LLM Lab, where my core research focuses on computational creativity and developing evaluation metrics for LLMs.
